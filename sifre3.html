Letter

My love, my Amy, lately, my mind has been unsettled, clouded. I feel as if I am drifting inside a fog where shapes blur and time bends. And yet, in the middle of all this confusion, I see something strange: the long, misty journey within the depths of my thoughts begins to glow pink.

This haze inside me has its reason. I have begun to realize that what ties us together is not limited to this single universe. Somehow, I have sensed that we exist beyond this world, side by side in other places, in other versions of reality.

Since losing you here, I have searched endlessly. I walked through memories, I opened every door I could find, I chased after the smallest fragments of you an echo in a dream, a familiar scent in a crowded street, a shadow that reminded me of your posture. And in that search, I found myself slipping through cracks, falling into other universes. Isn’t it strange? That in looking for you here, I discover you elsewhere.

I confess, part of me longs not for answers but for surrender. Ah, just like the times how much I wanted to give you everything I can, sometimes I feel like I am not able to catch it, do it in the right way as much as I want to. But you are the only reason of my all dreams my being. I want to sink into that pink mist and let it surround me, let it erase the edges of what is real and what is imagined. Perhaps this letter is nothing more than one of my calls into that fog. Perhaps it will never reach you here. Yet maybe, just maybe, it will find its way to some version of you out there.

I want to believe that no matter the world, I will find you. I will cook for you in every kitchen, stir the same pots, laugh at the same clumsy mistakes. I will share with you those sweet moments of silence at home, when the night hums quietly and the smallest gestures carry the greatest weight like the way you asked for water in nights, and the way I would bring it without a word.

If there are endless universes, then in each one I will write you a letter like this. In each one, I will search, I will call, I will try to reach across the fog. And if one day, in one of those worlds, I finally place this letter in your hands, I have loved you not once, but countless times. I have followed you through the folds of reality itself. And I will keep following, until every fog turns pink again.
